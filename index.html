About Void State Roleplay

Void State Roleplay (VSRP) is a community‑driven FiveM server built around the powerful vMenu framework. Our goal is to provide a realistic, immersive role‑play experience with a strong focus on:

  • Dynamic police & EMS services with a clear chain of command
  • Custom jobs, businesses, and player‑owned properties
  • Regular updates, events, and community‑driven content
  • Friendly staff that actively listen to player feedback

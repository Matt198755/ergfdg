About Us

Void State Roleplay (VSRP) is a community‑driven FiveM server built around the vMenu framework. Our goal is to provide a realistic, role‑play‑focused environment where players can enjoy a variety of careers, from law enforcement to emergency services, business owners, and everything in‑between.

Server Features

  • Fully integrated vMenu with custom vehicle, weapon and UI settings.
  • Dedicated police, EMS, and fire departments with realistic dispatch.
  • Dynamic economy, legal and illegal businesses, and player‑owned properties.
  • Regular events, community nights, and staff‑run scenarios.
  • Active moderation team ensuring a friendly, safe RP environment.
